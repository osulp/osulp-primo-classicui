Library Resources - Search Scopes

The catalog has 6 scopes, or filters, that allow a user to search specific combinations of our collections. You can select a search scope in the Library Resources tab dropdown menu.

OSU Libraries

Search for materials held by all OSU Libraries, including Corvallis, Newport, and Bend locations.

OSU + Summit Libraries

Expand your search to the collections of OSU and the participating libraries of Summit, part of the Orbis Cascade Alliance.

Bend (OSU Cascades + COCC)

Search for materials held at the Barber Library in Bend - a shared facility of the OSU-Cascades branch campus and Central Oregon Community College.

Special Collections and Archives

Search our unique collections of manuscripts, archives, photographs, books and more from SCARC.

Articles

Search the collections from many of the electronic resources and databases to which OSU subscribes.

EBSCO

Focuses on articles from our EBSCO databases. You can also search these databases individually from our databases page.

Course Reserves

Use the Course Reserves tab to search for items on reserve. You may search by course name, number, instructor, or by title. Use the facets on the left to narrow down your results. Additionally, you may click on the 'Details' tab of the record and see course information in the Course Information section of the record display.

How do I...

Find out more about what OSU Libraries offer with Library DIY, Research Guides, and Tutorials. For more specific searching, try one of our many subject-specific databases. To broaden your options, search libraries worldwide in WorldCat and request materials from Interlibrary Loan (ILL).
