Sign In to 1Search

OSU students, faculty, and staff can sign in to 1Search for access to additional resources and services. These include:

  • Access additional full-text content from more journals, databases (e.g., Web of Science) and e-books.
  • Borrow books from Summit libraries.
  • Easily access and save items and searches to your account.
