Refine Your Results

Use the facet to refine your search.


Start with a broad search and then refine it. Options for refining your search are located on the left side of the screen.


You can refine your search by Resources Type, Topic, Author, Date and to a specific library.


 

Click on the More options link to see more refining options.

Search Scopes

1Search has 6 scopes, or filters, that allow you to search specific areas of our collections. Click on the drop-down menu in the 1Search tab to choose a different scope.

Books, media and articles

This is the default scope. Use this scope to locate materials in the OSU Libraries, in Summit, journals, journal articles, media, and many other resources.

At OSU Libraries only

Search for materials (books, journals, audio-visual materials, maps, government documents, etc.) held by all OSU Libraries, including Corvallis, Newport (Guin), and Bend (OSU Cascades) locations. This is similar to our old library catalog.

OSU Libraries + Summit

Expand your search to the collections of OSU and the participating 36 academic libraries of the Orbis-Cascade Alliance (formerly Summit catalog). This scope does not include journal articles.

Special Collections and Archives

Search our unique collections of manuscripts, archives, photographs, rare books ,and more from SCARC.

Articles

Search the collections from many of the electronic resources and databases to which OSU subscribes. For more specific searching, try one of our many research databases.

OSU Cascades

Search for OSU-owned materials held at the Barber Library in Bend - a shared facility of the OSU-Cascades branch campus and Central Oregon Community College.
